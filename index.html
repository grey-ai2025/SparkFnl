Strategic Recommendations

Immediate Actions (Next 30 Days)

Critical Risk Mitigation

High-Priority Training: Deploy intensive training for the 12 employees scoring below 40, focusing on Local vs. Cloud Hosting and Advanced Retrieval literacy.

Team Support: Provide additional resources to Operations and Sales teams showing concentrated risk areas.

Manager Coaching: Implement targeted support for Kevin Zhang (Operations) and Robert Thompson (Sales) to improve team performance.

Medium Term Strategy (Next 60 Days)

Capability Building

AI Champion Program: Leverage the 18 high performers as internal trainers and mentors for their respective teams.

Category-Specific Training: Launch focused learning paths for the bottom 3 categories: Local vs. Cloud Hosting, Advanced Retrieval, and Data Structuring.

Cross-Team Learning: Facilitate knowledge sharing between high-performing IT/Marketing teams and lower-performing Operations/Sales teams.

Longer Term Strategy (Next 90 Days)

Organizational Transformation

Competency Standards: Establish minimum AI literacy scores of 65+ for all employees, with role-specific requirements.

Continuous Assessment: Implement quarterly SPARK assessments to track progress and identify emerging skill gaps.

Integration with Performance Management: Include AI literacy scores in annual reviews and career development planning.
